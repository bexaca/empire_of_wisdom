Psi su deo našeg života

Odgajivačnica EMPIRE OF WISDOM osnovana je 2010. godine pod rednim FCI brojem 5042. Nalazimo se u Srbiji, Novoj Pazovi, na samo 30 km od Beograda i 40 km od Novog Sada. Iza sebe imamo bogato kinološko iskustvo u uzgoju pasa jos iz davnih 90-ih godina, da bi se 2008. godine odlučili za rasu Cane Corso I 2010. godine je zvanično registrovali i tada počinje priča o našoj odgajivačnici što dokazuju naši rezultati i uspesi pasa iz našeg uzgoja svuda po svetu. Uspešno sarađujemo sa ostalim odgajivačima kao i sa našim partnerom PROTECTION DOG WORLDWIDE, koja se bavi obukom pasa za zaštitu i čuvanje lica i objekata iz Velike Britanije koja nam je ukazala poverenje i imala uspeha sa mnogim našim psima.

Introducing Novi Sad

Welcome to Novi Sad, European Capital of Culture! Vibrant and colorful city with a long and dynamic history. When in Novi Sad, you can fill your days with all sorts of activities. Whether it's walking and exploring colorful streets of Old Town, walking along a beautiful Danube quay, or visiting galleries and museums of Novi Sad. Also, you can cross the Duga bridge and enjoy a breathtaking landscape on the historical Petrovaradin fortress, that overlooks the city as well as the National Park of Fruska Gora. Because of its rich and multi-national history, you can hear a couple of different languages while walking amongst our friendly cohabitants. Whether you visit us in the summer or winter, you can enjoy yourself on one of our heavenly beaches or magnificent mountains.

Hiking

Besides being beneficial for health, hiking is a great source of pleasure. Once you leave the crowded streets and traffic, you enter a stress-free zone, surrounded by amazing scenery and nature. Feel free to contact us about any information or booking. Remember, we are at Your service.

Biking

Due to its structure and geographical position, Novi Sad is considered an ideal city for a bicycle. You can choose to explore the city on it, as well as getting some fresh air and exersise. The power and simplicity of two weels is just amazing!

Camping

Relief yourself from stress and hectic city life by spending a night by a cosy fire under the stars. Experience the outdoors at it's finest, feel the nature and freedom of many beautiful campsites around Novi Sad, and further.

River boating

We are proud to have the greatest European river go through Novi Sad, and outlines the city lines in the best possible way. Join us as we embark you on a boat trip exploring wild beaches. Are you a gourmet? Prepare your pallets for new and exquisite traditional Serbian dishes, enjoying on taverns with gazing view of the powerful Danube.
